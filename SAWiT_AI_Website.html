About SAWiT.AI

SAWiT.AI is the world’s largest women-only Gen AI movement. In collaboration with GUVI and South Asia Women in Tech (SAWiT), our mission is to upskill and empower women in the field of Artificial Intelligence, bridging the gender gap and driving innovation.

Why Join Us?

✔ Participate in AI Learnathons and Hackathons
✔ Gain hands-on experience with leading AI tools
✔ Network with mentors and industry leaders
✔ Celebrate achievements at the SAWiT.AI Festival
✔ Unlock career opportunities in tech!
